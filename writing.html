Creative Writing

In the creative writing event, participants will have a day to respond to writing prompts that have tied connections with the IGCSE curriculums in the subjects of Literature, Maths, Sciences, and History. With this prompt, they are free to write in any form they wish in order to express their ideas creatively (eg. poems, speeches, songs...)!
