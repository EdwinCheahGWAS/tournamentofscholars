About the organiser

Hey there! I am Edwin Cheah, a student studying at XCL World Academy Singapore. I started this initiative as my personal project because of my passion for education and my joy from helping fellow students. This initiative was inspired by my own experiences in academic competitions and through the realisation that motivation to learn is essential yet lacking in many of our learning journeys. Therefore, I have started the Tournament of Scholars to attempt to help you realise the importance of the knowledge you learn in school in real life applications in a fun way!
