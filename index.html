What exactly is the Tournament of Scholars?

The Tournament of Scholars is a free competition that aims to promote education in a fun and engaging way. Based off knowledge from the IGCSE syllabus, participants will complete knowledge tests and creative writing tasks.

What makes the Tournament of Scholars so special?

Resources

This competition is accompanied by incredible resources that cover the curriculum of the Tournament of Scholars. This allows participants to learn with accessible and high-quality resources in several subjects containing a translation feature to be internationally-friendly.
