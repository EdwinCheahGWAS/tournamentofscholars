Quizzes

In the quiz event, you will be tested a variety of questions that test your knowledge of the IGCSE currciulum in History, Maths, and Sciences. Each individual is tested in their critical thinking skills as well as knowledge in a 60 question multiple choice quiz which has to be completed over the course of 45 minutes. Questions will award a quantity of points that reflect the difficulty and thinking required in the question. Out of 5 choices, scholars will pick the most fitting choice that they believe is correct.
